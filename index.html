About

A practical space for understanding sustainability.

terra.guide is built by Lilit — an instructional designer and product manager. The platform exists because sustainability is genuinely complicated, and the goal here is simple: publish everything jargon-free and practical, so that anyone can pick up a resource and do something useful with it.

Every resource is anchored to a real regulation or framework, and reviewed when things change.
